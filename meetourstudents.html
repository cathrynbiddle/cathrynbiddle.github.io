At GMA we are proud to have a diverse student body. Families, men, women and children of all ages and ethnicities train together on mats. We have been fortunate to train people with both physical and mental disabilities, ranging from ADHD and Autism to stroke victims and wounded veterans. In every case, the GMA family has been a valuable tool that helps encourage the success of every student.

GMA programs are available to all age groups and can be more specifically catered to the individual goals of each student-- from the baby boomer wanting an interesting form of exercise, to the 30 year old woman who wants the peace of mind that comes from self-defense training, to the child with ADD whose doctor prescribed martial arts classes. Couple our instructors' expertise with a client intensive approach to service, and we believe GMA can provide a martial arts education superior to most competitors.
