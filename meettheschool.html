The Gentry of the western Renaissance sought to combine
the knowledge of the classical Greeks with their own era's
approach to philosophy and life.

We at Gentry Martial Arts likewise seek to marry the traditional
martial spirit to modern approaches of technique and conditioning.
We believe a complete martial artist has as much strength of mind
and character as strength of body.

We welcome you to our home and hope you will join us in appreciating
the martial arts as a means of developing the whole individual.

Our name is an explicit reference to one of the main objectives of
GMA's curriculum - the development of better people.

Gentry Martial Arts is located at 1909 E. Morgan St. Martinsville, IN 46151.

We are next door to Martinsville's YMCA and across the street from a community favorite, The Inside Scoop. To find us on the map, click here.

About the facility:

Our facility is designed to be functional for the student and
accommodating and comfortable for the parent.
At approximately 5000 square feet, it is one of the largest
and nicest martial arts facilities in the state.
We offer men's and women's changing rooms, a large and fully
matted training floor, an additional wooden-floored training room,
a full service Pro Shop, and a large viewing area.
