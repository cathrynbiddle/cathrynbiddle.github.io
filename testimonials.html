These organizations have requirements that are consistent with GMA's reasons for being and facilitate the process Tagore described as making “vitally savage and mentally civilized.” Tae Kwon Do – Kukki-taekwondo and Chungdokwan Hapkido – United States Hapkido Federation and Korea Hapkido Federation Tai Chi Chu'an – New School Tai Chi Association and William CC Chen's Yang style Tai Chi Chu'uan Brazilian Jiu Jitsu – Small Axe Jiu Jitsu and Team Atos through Andre Galvao Chinna (Chinese joint locking) – Dr. yang Jwing Ming Jeet Kune Do and Kali – Martial Concepts FAST Self Defense – FAST Defense Global GMA Reasons for Being 1. To provide the highest quality martial arts and self-defense instruction in the most professional atmosphere possible. 2. To promote a more complete martial arts education through a cross-disciplinary approach to the academic subject matter needed to broaden and deepen one's martial arts understanding. 3. To foster a practice environment that is not only safe, but also cements social ties among its membership. 4. To utilize the martial arts as a vehicle for self-cultivation, which includes increased fitness and mental health, a better understanding of self, and an improved sense of responsibility consistent with the ethos of traditional martial arts.
